ABOUT FRONTIER

Frontier Biscuit Factory was established in 1921 in West Pakistan by Late Mr. Mangal Sain which was later on continued by his son Mr. Munshi Ram Sethi. After partition, Mr. Sethi shifted his business to India and continued its ancestral profession by setting a small production unit at Sadar Bazar, Delhi. Slowly and gradually, it gained acceptability and popularity. Encouraged by ever-increasing response from customers at large, it has established two Processing Zones in Delhi & Haryana. Frontier is now being recognized as a leading manufacturer of ‘Eggless Premium Biscuits’. It has honestly maintained a track record of serving self-developed ‘Eggless Premium Biscuits’ through its Exclusive Shopping Zones with the aim of providing high quality products and services to its customers. Frontier has strived to set new standards of quality in all of its operations.
